MISSION

With the Divine grace and guidance, the Palawan Bethel Senior High School (PBSHS), a Bible-believing Christian School, is committed to the service of youth, country, and God.

PBSHS upholds lifelong learning through progressive, creative, innovative, and relevant instruction providing balance between academics and student activities in molding students to be God-loving, humane, just, competent, and service oriented.

VISION

The PBSHS, strives to develop humane, just, morally upright, competent, and service-oriented individuals who will lead, nurture and pass on these ideals to the next generation in its aim to serve the youth, country, and God with utmost competence, professionalism, and commitment.

CORE VALUES

Benevolence, Excellence, Thankfulness, Honesty & Integrity, Environment-Friendliness, Loyalty, Love of God and Country, Service, Hard work, Social Responsibility
